What I Do

Research, Build, Teach

Research

Studying health technology adoption and the behavioral factors that shape it. Current focus: telehealth in Indonesia, specifically how socioeconomic context, perceived risk, and trust interact with technology uptake decisions. Dissertation in progress (N=480, three cities). One paper under review at Information Research.

Building

I design and build AI systems informed by behavioral science. Built Caesar, an AI agent that applies behavioral frameworks (Fogg, SDT, TMT, COR) as architectural constraints, not just metaphors. Deployed on Google Cloud.

Teaching & Mentoring

Ten years in higher education at Institut Teknologi Kalimantan, East Kalimantan. Led the institution's first international accreditation (ASIIN, 8 programs). Secured curriculum development grants. Currently mentoring junior lecturers and doctoral students informally.

Projects

Active Work

Live prototype

Caesar

An AI agent that uses behavioral science frameworks — Fogg's Behavior Model, Self-Determination Theory, Terror Management Theory, and Conservation of Resources Theory — as architectural logic, not just design decoration. Built using OpenClaw on a Google Cloud VM.

Python Google Cloud OpenClaw Behavioral AI
Under Review

Voices from Reddit — Generative AI and Health

Mixed-methods analysis of 6,707 Reddit posts examining how people discuss generative AI in health contexts. Combines computational text analysis with qualitative coding to identify patterns in public perception, concern, and use.

Python Reddit API NLP Mixed Methods
Data collection in progress

Dissertation — Telehealth Indonesia

Cross-sectional survey study (N=480, three Indonesian cities) applying the Health Belief Model to understand what drives and blocks telehealth adoption. Tests nine hypotheses relating perceived severity, benefits, barriers, self-efficacy, and cues to action.

HBM Survey Research SEM
In progress

Reddit Telehealth Perceptions (HBM)

Directed qualitative content analysis of Reddit discussions about telehealth, using the Health Belief Model as the coding framework. Examines how lay health beliefs expressed in online communities map onto established behavioral constructs.

Qualitative Content Analysis HBM
Live / In development

Agentic AI Pipeline — Reddit Health

A Python-based agentic pipeline that automates the collection and initial analysis of health-related Reddit data. Connects Reddit API output to LLM-based processing for theme identification and behavioral pattern detection.

Python LLM Reddit API Health NLP
